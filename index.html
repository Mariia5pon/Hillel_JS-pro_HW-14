14.1. Slider

Write your own image slider that should:

  • Display the image and the Next, Prev buttons on the sides of the image.
  • When you click on Next, the next image is displayed.
  • When you click on Prev - the previous one.
  • When the last image is reached, hide the Next button. Do the same with the first image and the Prev button.
  • The number of slides can be any.
  • Add the ability to navigate through the points under the slides.
